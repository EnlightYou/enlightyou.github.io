WHAT WE DO

At EnlightYou, the services we provide are a product of our team’s collaborative efforts.

Private and Group Tutoring

Our tutors will teach any subject of your choosing and will adapt their teaching style to provide you the best educational experience.

Examination and Board Reviews

Whether it be for school examinations or board preparation, our team will prepare and host lecture-style review per your class’s request.

Personalized Mentorship

Our mentors create individualized, step-by-step plans together with students that enables them to realize their goals.

Research Opportunities

Working alongside our fellow medical and educational colleagues, we provide avenues and opportunities for our students to partake in research of their interest.

Career Development

Whether it be applying for college or residency, our team offers personal statement editing, interview preparation, and CV/resume editing services to help you stand out from the crowd.

Community Service Opportunities

Every month, we donate a percentage of our proceeds to charity. Moreover, we are currently creating volunteering opportunities across to globe. Join the EnlightYou family in promoting humanity!

01.

CONSULTATION

The EnlightYou team strives to provides personalized guidance specifically tailored for each student. During the initial consult with one of our educational directors, we take the time to appreciate our student’s goals, definition of success, and personality to tailor our services to their particular needs.

02.

CONNECTION

  After developing a clear understanding of our students and their ambitions, we connect them with a member of the EnlightYou team. We specifically match together an EnlightYou mentor whose teaching style complements the learnings-style of the student.

03.

COLLABORATION

The EnlightYou mentor provides personalized mentorship and instruction on any topics of interest. We strive to ensure that the EnlightYou mentor-mentee relationship is continuously maturing. We constantly reach out for feedback so our team can improve and exceed our students’ expectations.
